Stream Syllabus

I.A. (Arts)

Compulsory: English or Hindi
Electives (Choose 3): History, Geography, Political Science, Economics, Sociology.
Optional/Additional: Regional Languages / Sanskrit.

I.Sc. (Science)

Compulsory: English or Hindi
Core subjects: Physics, Chemistry
Math/Bio Branch: Mathematics AND/OR Biology. (Computer Science available as optional).

I.Com. (Commerce)

Compulsory: English or Hindi
Core subjects: Accountancy, Business Studies.
Electives (Choose 1): Economics, Commercial Arithmetic, Entrepreneurship.
